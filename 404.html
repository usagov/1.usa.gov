The 1.USA.gov URL shortening service is no longer supported and therefore can’t send you to the page you were looking for.

  • Español

If you clicked a 1.USA.gov URL

Here’s what you can try:

  1. If you know the website you were trying to get to originally, you can go directly there and use the search box to try to find the content you were looking for.
  2. If you still can’t find what you are looking for, you can visit USA.gov to continue your search.

If you created a 1.USA.gov URL

The creation of new 1.usa.gov links hasn’t been possible for nearly three years. Please stop using 1.usa.gov URLs in your messages.

El servicio de 1.USA.gov para abreviar URLs ya no está disponible, por lo tanto, no puede enviarlo a la página que estaba buscando.
